Collect magazines without the clutter.

Subscriber lets you subscribe to magazines and download them to your device. Syncing your downloaded magazines to the Cloud lets you read them on any of your devices, making it easier than ever to sit back, relax, and enjoy the read.

Sign Up

Downloading Subscriber allows you to easily search for a specific magazine, or casually browse as you look for something new to read. Once you find a magazine that you like, simply fill out the subscription form and download the magazine to your device. Depending on which version of subscriber you get, you could save up to 20% on your magazine subscriptions. The 3 different versions of Subscriber ensure that you get exactly what you need.
